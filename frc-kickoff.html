About

FRC Kickoff marks the beginning of the design and build season for High School students involved in FIRST. At this event, teams will the challenge and rules for the 2019 game at Purdue University's Neil Armstrong Hall of Engineering.

Transportation & Parking

Cars, Pickups, and Vans can park in either the University (201 N University St) or the Northwestern (504 Northwestern Avenue) Parking Garage and walk to Lawson.

Buses can drop off and pick up on the stree outside Lawson. Bus parking is in the large lot north of Ross-Ade stadium. Brittany Ammerman (609-744-5955) will be available to shuttle ADULT DRIVERS back and forth from the bus parking area.
