About



Event hosted by Purdue FIRST Programs in coordination with Indiana FIRST to teach FRC and FTC teams about technical mentorship aspects of FIRST Programs. Hands-on workshops hosted on campus at Purdue University in Armstrong, Forney, and Physics for team mentors, students, and supporters to participate in.
