About



Be a part of the second Boiler Bot Battle, hosted at Purdue University by Purdue FIRST Programs. Join us at the Purdue Armory on November 10th and 11th for robots, campus tours, social events, and much more. Check back to this page for frequent updates, and we hope to see you in November! .
Got questions about the event? Send them to boilerbotbattle@gmail.com!

Team Social


The team social will take place on Friday, November 10th from 6:00-10:00pm. The social will cost $7.50 per person attending and will include...
- Bowling
- Pool (Billiards)
- Board Games
- A $10 meal ticket for dinner at a Purdue Memorial Union restaurant
- Campus tours
- Fun for all!
