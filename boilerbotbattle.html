About



Be a part of the fourth annual Boiler Bot Battle, hosted at Purdue University by Purdue FIRST Programs. Join us at the Purdue Armory on November 15th and 16th for robots, campus tours, social events, and much more. Check back to this page for frequent updates, and we hope to see you in November!

Got questions about the event? Send them to boilerbotbattle@gmail.com!
